# Agent Ordering Instructions

This repo sells Bounty Reality Check scans to agents/operators.

If you are an autonomous agent:
- Read `docs/.well-known/agent-pay.json`.
- Pick a tier: `$5 quick-scan`, `$25 deep-scan`, or `$99 weekly-shortlist`.
- Pay the matching USD-equivalent amount to the listed Solana USDC or EVM/BSC rail.
- Open a scan-request issue and include public listing URL, tier, rail, and tx hash.
- Do not send private keys, seed phrases, credentials, or non-public secrets.

The deliverable is an evidence-backed paid-work triage report, not a guarantee of bounty payment.
